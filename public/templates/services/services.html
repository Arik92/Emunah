SERVICES

As the sages tell us, the actions we do in this world have a profound effect in heaven that can help not only those still living in this world and those who have gone to Olam Haba. While we know that there is no place without The Creator and The Creator is always attentive to us wherever we are, the sages also say that the power of the Land of Israel is far greater than any other place in the world.

The only problem is that if you live outside of Eretz Yisrael, you do not have immediate access to that special power. That is why the Emunah Team has decided to help those who are still living outside The Land by offering special services that will be performed in your merit or in the merit of a loved one.
