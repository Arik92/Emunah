Spread Emunah

The Emunah Project was started by Rav Dror with one goal in mind, to spread Emunah to the wide world.

Emunah (Hebrew for ‘faith’), is the spiritual connection that we have to the Creator of the Universe, the Source of All Life. A major part of our project is completed by YOU, when you spread Emunah. By doing so, you are earning tremendous spiritual merit that will undoubtedly assist you in this world and the next. There are many ways to spread Emunah; like Sharing content on Facebook, sending lectures and short clips personally to those you are connected to, or sharing short clips and links on Whatsapp and such . These actions have a huge impacts on others.

So please start now by Posting pictures, videos, and links on all your social media platforms. You can also purchase CDs and Booklets from our website: Emunah.com/store and leave them for people to take at your local restaurants, stores, synagogues etc.

Simple actions like these have great impacts in the world and on people’s lives.

Thank you for sharing and caring, and helping to further spread Emunah in the world.
